Discord Rules

Chat Rules

➜ Keep chat tidy and civilized.

➜ No politics or discussion about war(s).

➜ No profanity in messages, images and memes.

➜ No advertising other servers.

➜ Be kind to all members. Any form of bullying or unwanted negativity will not be tolerated.

➜ Keep topics related to Minecraft and/or the CatCraft server. All other conversations may be made in #off-topic.

➜ Posts in #shameless-self-promotion must be related to CatCraft in some way. You 'may' post a few times if promoting your social media channel(s), however, they must abide by the Rules. If in doubt, contact staff through a support ticket.

➜ Messages on the discord server may be removed at any time if staff believe it is in violation of the chat rules.

➜ Player suggestions that are unrealistic or receive an overwhelming number of dislikes (i.e. a ratio >90% from players) may be removed from the channel by the Administrators.

➜ Do not ask to be staff. You may apply when applications are open at #staff-applications.

➜ Do not spam the same or similar message and/or emoji multiple times in a short timeframe.

➜ Speculation of a player that may be hacking must not be discussed in any channel. If you would like to report a player or a member of the staff team, please create a ticket at #staff-help or email admin@catcraft.net

➜ If you have been temporarily banned from the server, do not ask to be unbanned. You must appeal your ban at https://catcraft.net/banappeal

➜ Do not ping the administrators. If you would like to contact staff, please create a support ticket.

Discord Profile Rules

➜ No Inappropriate names or profile pictures.

➜ No alternative accounts.

➜ No Impersonation of another member or staff.

➜ No hate speech in your status.

➜ Make sure you adhere to discord's TOS https://discord.com/guidelines

About Us

CatCraft is a Minecraft: Java and Bedrock Edition server that aims to expand and enhance vanilla survival gameplay. CatCraft runs on the latest version of Minecraft and is continually updating to bring breathtaking new features all year round. If you love survival Minecraft and Cats, this is the server for you!
